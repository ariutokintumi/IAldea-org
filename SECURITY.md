# Security

IAldea handles community-facing data and policy. If you believe you have found a **security vulnerability** (privacy leak, bypass of policy, unsafe ingestion, etc.):

1. **Do not** open a public issue with exploit details.
2. Contact the maintainers through the channels published on [ialdea.org](https://ialdea.org/) or the ETH Cinco de Mayo project contacts in the README.

We will acknowledge receipt when possible and coordinate disclosure.

For **dependency or supply-chain** issues, follow the same private reporting path.
